\section{Task 1}

There are 13 covergroup bins that are written in the \texttt{covergroup fifo\_cov} block.
The Table \ref{tab:fifo_bins} describe better the various bins.

The directive is expicit in the property block \texttt{becomes\_empty\_after\_read}.

\begin{table}[H]
	\centering
	\begin{tabular}{@{}lll@{}}
		\toprule
		\textbf{Coverpoint}         	& \textbf{Bins}	& \textbf{Names} 																	\\ \midrule
		\texttt{full}               	& 1 	        & \texttt{went\_full} 																\\
		\texttt{empty}              	& 1 	        & \texttt{went\_empty} 																\\
		\texttt{dut.count}          	& 3 	        & \texttt{empty\_bin}, \texttt{mid\_bins}, \texttt{full\_bin} 						\\
		\texttt{wr\_en}             	& 2 	        & 0 and 1 (Implicit) 																\\
		\texttt{rd\_en}             	& 2 	        & 0 and 1 (Implicit) 																\\
		\texttt{Cross wr\_en, rd\_en}	& 4 	        & \texttt{write\_only}, \texttt{read\_only}, \texttt{both\_off}, \texttt{illegal} 	\\ \midrule
		\textbf{Total Bins}         	& \textbf{13}   & 																					\\ \bottomrule
	\end{tabular}
